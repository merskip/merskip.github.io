Gothic 1 Remake - Lockpicking solver

To open the lock in Gothic 1 Rename, you need to solve a puzzle. It involves arranging all sliders so that every pin aligns on a single line at position 4 (the&snexact center). The difficulty comes from the feedback coupling between the sliders - when you move one slider, others may change their positions at the same time.

Step-by-step

  1. Start with slider 1 (yellow color) and enter its position (white color) into the "Initial state" field. Then, after a comma, enter the remaining sliders in order.
    In this example: 5, 6, 1, 3, 2, 1
  2. Now you need to analyze how sliders influence each other. Simply move a selected slider in any direction and observe how the other sliders respond. Each row in the "Slider effects" section corresponds to a specific slider.

    Enter the numbers of the sliders that move together with the selected one:
    If a slider moves in the same direction, write it as a positive number.
    If it moves in the opposite direction, write it as a negative number.

    For example, in this case:
    when you move slider 1, then slider 4 moves in the opposite direction,
    so in the field "Slider 1" enter "-4".
  3. After entering the initial state and all dependencies, click "Solve".

    If you did not make any mistakes and a solution exists, the solution will be displayed below. Follow the provided instructions step by step. The arrows indicate the direction in which each slider should be moved.
